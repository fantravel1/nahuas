Factual accuracy is a core value at Nahuas.com, and every correction helps strengthen the site.

Native and heritage Nahuatl speakers are especially valued contributors. We need help with pronunciation recordings, dialect documentation, dictionary entries, grammar verification, translation work, and the creation of learning materials. Whether you speak Classical Nahuatl or a modern variant from Puebla, Veracruz, the Huasteca, or elsewhere, your knowledge is invaluable. Please contact us using the form above or email us directly at info@nahuas.com.
Nahuas.com is an independent educational platform.
