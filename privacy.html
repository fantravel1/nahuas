Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

  1. Home
  2. Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

At Nahuas.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your personal data. Nahuas.com is a free educational platform dedicated to Nahua civilization, Nahuatl language preservation, and cultural understanding. We believe that access to knowledge should not come at the cost of your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use our contact form, you may voluntarily provide personal information such as your name, email address, and the content of your message. We collect this information only when you choose to provide it and solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit Nahuas.com, certain technical information may be collected automatically by our web server and analytics tools. This may include:

  • Your IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Language preferences
  • Date and time of your visit

This information is used in aggregate to understand how visitors use our site and to improve the user experience. We do not use this data to personally identify individual visitors.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Responding to Inquiries: When you contact us through our contact form, we use your name and email address to respond to your message.
  • Improving Our Platform: Aggregated, anonymized analytics data helps us understand which content is most valuable to our visitors, identify technical issues, and prioritize improvements.
  • Language Preferences: We store your selected language preference (English, Spanish, or Nahuatl) in your browser's local storage so that the site displays in your preferred language on return visits.
  • Site Security: Technical data may be used to detect and prevent abuse, unauthorized access, or other security threats.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We will never use your data in ways that are incompatible with the purposes described in this policy.

3. Cookies

Nahuas.com uses a minimal number of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the basic functioning of the website, such as remembering your language preference. They do not track your browsing activity across other sites.
  • Analytics Cookies: If we use web analytics tools, they may place cookies on your device to help us understand site usage patterns. These cookies collect anonymized data and do not identify you personally.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of the site, such as language preference persistence.

4. Third-Party Services

Nahuas.com integrates a limited number of third-party services to enhance functionality and content delivery:

YouTube Embeds

Some pages may include embedded video content from YouTube. When you view a page containing a YouTube embed, YouTube may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policy. We use YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode where available to minimize data collection. For details, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to serve the typefaces displayed on this website (Cormorant Garamond and Inter). When you visit Nahuas.com, your browser may make requests to Google's servers to download font files. Google may collect limited technical information as part of this process. For details, please review Google Fonts Privacy Information.

We carefully evaluate any third-party service before integrating it and strive to minimize the amount of data shared with external providers.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information collected through Nahuas.com. These measures include:

  • Serving the site over HTTPS to encrypt data in transit
  • Regularly updating our web server software and dependencies
  • Limiting access to personal data to authorized team members who need it to respond to inquiries
  • Not storing sensitive data beyond what is necessary to fulfill the stated purposes

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to implementing best practices to safeguard your data.

6. Children's Privacy

Nahuas.com is an educational resource designed to be accessible to a wide audience, including students and young learners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information to us through our contact form, please contact us at info@nahuas.com and we will promptly delete that information.

We encourage parents and educators to guide young learners in their use of online resources and to help them understand the importance of not sharing personal information without parental consent.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal data, subject to any legal obligations to retain it.
  • Objection: You may object to certain types of processing of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: Where applicable, you may request that your data be provided in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@nahuas.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Continued use of Nahuas.com after changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

✉ Privacy Inquiries

Email: info@nahuas.com
Contact Form: nahuas.com/contact
Subject Line: Privacy Policy Inquiry

We are committed to resolving any concerns you may have about our data practices promptly and transparently.
