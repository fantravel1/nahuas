About This Collection

About These Videos

Every video on this page has been hand-selected from trusted educational channels including Khan Academy, Crash Course, TED-Ed, and Overly Sarcastic Productions. These creators are known for their rigorous research, engaging presentation, and commitment to accuracy. Together, they offer a rich multimedia introduction to the Nahua world — from the rise of the Aztec Empire and the cosmic myths of the Five Suns, to the daily rhythms of life in Tenochtitlan and the sophisticated knowledge systems that underpinned Mesoamerican civilization.
